STEP 2 — IMPACT SCORE  (impact_score)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Count every bullet point or dash-prefixed line under Experience, Projects, or Work sections.
Call this TOTAL_BULLETS.

Count QUANTIFIED_BULLETS: bullets containing at least one of:

If TOTAL_BULLETS = 0: impact_score = 0
If TOTAL_BULLETS > 0:
  raw = round((QUANTIFIED_BULLETS / TOTAL_BULLETS) * 100)
  If raw = 0 and TOTAL_BULLETS > 0: impact_score = 10   (has bullets, none quantified)
